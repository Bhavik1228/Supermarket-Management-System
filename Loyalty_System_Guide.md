# Loyalty System Guide: Coupons & Gamification

Welcome to the Supermarket Loyalty System! This guide explains how customers can earn rewards and how staff can process them.

---

## 1. Coupon System

Coupons are special discounts that can be applied to orders. They can be earned through points, won in games, or gifted by the store.

### For Customers: How to get & use coupons
1.  **Redeeming Points**: Go to the **Loyalty Dashboard** and click on "Rewards". You can exchange your accumulated points for various coupons (e.g., "10% Off" or "$5 Flat Discount").
2.  **Claiming Coupons**: Once redeemed, the coupon will appear in your **My Coupons** section.
3.  **Checkout**: Show your coupon code (or loyalty QR code) to the cashier at the checkout counter.

### For Cashiers: How to apply coupons
1.  Scan the customer's items as usual in the **POS Interface**.
2.  Search for the customer by name, email, or scan their loyalty card.
3.  Click the **"Apply Coupon"** button.
4.  Enter the coupon code provided by the customer.
5.  The system will automatically:
    *   Verify the coupon is active and not expired.
    *   Check if the order meet the **Minimum Spend** requirement.
    *   Apply the discount to the total.
6.  Complete the transaction. The coupon will be marked as "Used" in the system.

---

## 2. Gamified System (Spin the Wheel)

To keep shopping fun, we've introduced the **Daily Spin**!

### How it works:
1.  **Daily Access**: Every 24 hours (or as configured by the store), you can visit the **"Games"** section in your Loyalty Dashboard.
2.  **The Spin**: Click the **Spin** button to start the wheel.
3.  **Prizes**: You can win:
    *   **Bonus Points**: Instantly added to your balance.
    *   **Exclusive Coupons**: High-value discounts specifically for game winners.
    *   **Better Luck**: If you don't win today, come back tomorrow!
4.  **Cooldown**: After spinning, a timer will show you when you can spin again.

---

## 3. Tier Rewards & Multipliers

Remember that your **Tier** (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum) affects how fast you earn points:
*   **Gold Members**: Earn 1.5x points on every dollar spent.
*   **Platinum Members**: Earn 2x points on every dollar spent.

Coupons can often be "Stacked" with your tier benefits unless otherwise stated on the coupon description.

---

## FAQ

**Q: Can I use multiple coupons on one order?**
A: Most coupons are non-stackable. The POS will alert the cashier if a coupon cannot be combined with others.

**Q: My coupon code isn't working at the POS.**
A: Check the "Min Spend" requirement. Many coupons require a minimum purchase amount (e.g. $50) before they can be applied.

**Q: Do my Spin the Wheel coupons expire?**
A: Yes. Most game rewards expire within 7 days. Be sure to use them on your next visit!
